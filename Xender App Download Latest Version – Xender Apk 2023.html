Errors & Solution

Connectivity Issues

Sometimes users failed to connect the app between devices.

Transfer Failures

Files may occasionally fail to transfer completely or encounter errors.

Solution: Check the available storage space on both devices and try resending the files. Also, ensure the app is updated to the latest version.


Compatibility Issues

Some files may not be compatible with certain devices or may not open correctly.

Solution: Verify the file formats and ensure the respective apps capable of opening them are installed on the receiving device.
